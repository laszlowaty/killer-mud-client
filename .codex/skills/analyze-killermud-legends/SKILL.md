# Analyze KillerMUD Legends

Read `lore/` only. Query domains `legends`, `artifacts`, `religion`, and `history` with `.codex/skills/query-killermud-lore/scripts/query_lore.py`.

Treat the existence of a documented legend separately from the truth of its content. Preserve competing variants, narrators, regions, and source perspectives. Connect artifact descriptions and inscriptions to narratives through IDs rather than rewriting them as accepted history. Include record IDs and source references.

Do not resolve myths into facts, edit canonical lore, or generate area content.
