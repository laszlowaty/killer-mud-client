# Analyze KillerMUD Politics

Read `lore/` only. Query domains `politics`, `geography`, and `organizations` with `.codex/skills/query-killermud-lore/scripts/query_lore.py` and include relations around the requested anchor.

Distinguish actual control from territorial claims, reputation, historical rule, and inference. Describe spatial relationships only as precisely as the records allow. Surface hard and soft constraints, contested borders, missing links, and contradictory accounts. Include record IDs and source references.

Do not choose vnum ranges, create exits, edit lore, or generate an area.
