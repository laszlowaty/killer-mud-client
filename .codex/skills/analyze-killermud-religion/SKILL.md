# Analyze KillerMUD Religion

Read `lore/` only. Query domains `religion`, `legends`, `artifacts`, and `geography` with `.codex/skills/query-killermud-lore/scripts/query_lore.py`.

Separate evidence that a belief exists from evidence that its supernatural claim is true. Attribute teachings to their deity, cult, help entry, NPC, inscription, or artifact. Compare regional practices and conflicts without merging similarly named beings or traditions prematurely. Include record IDs and source references.

Do not declare theology canonical, edit records, or generate area content.
